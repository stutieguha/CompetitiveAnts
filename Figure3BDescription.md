This file pertains to figure 3B from the article "Macroscopic Dominance From Microscopic Extremes: Symmetry Breaking in Spatial Competition (Karamched, B., Ryan, S.D., and Guha, S.)".

**Summary:** Two ant colonies compete to claim dominance over a food source. Competitive factors include proximity to the food source, colony size, and the lack of fear displayed towards the competing colony (β). The file exemplifies how the effect of increasing β for a colony magnifies its advantage with respect to its change in proximity to the food source and its competing colony's greater size.
